About the Game

The 13th Call is a suspense-driven mobile interactive story. Players act as a late-night emergency dispatcher handling a disturbing call from a woman trapped inside a building’s hidden 13th floor. Using branching choices, archived clues, and escalating danger, players decide whom to trust and how the case will end.

The experience combines visual novel storytelling, atmospheric illustrated scenes, a clue board investigation system, and collectible endings that reveal deeper layers of the mystery.

Key Features

  • Branching mystery narrative with multiple endings
  • Choice-driven suspense and investigation
  • Illustrated scenes and visual novel presentation
  • Clue board system tracking truth, danger, panic, and trust
  • Ending archive and replay value
  • Short-session mobile friendly design

Description

Answer a call that should never have reached you.



The 13th Call is a suspenseful interactive story game where you play as a late-night emergency dispatcher. A frightened caller named Anna claims she is trapped on the 13th floor of a building that should not exist. As the line crackles and the situation grows more unstable, you must question witnesses, review records, inspect clues, and choose how to guide the caller to safety.



Investigate a branching mystery told through atmospheric scenes, illustrated character moments, and tension-filled decisions. Every call pushes you closer to the truth—or deeper into danger.



Features:

• Narrative-driven mystery with multiple endings

• Choice-based gameplay with meaningful consequences

• Dark, cinematic atmosphere with illustrated scenes

• Clue board system tracking truth, danger, panic, and trust

• Archive system to collect endings and uncover hidden layers of the case

• Designed for short, immersive play sessions on mobile



Will you uncover the truth behind the 13th floor before the line goes dead?
