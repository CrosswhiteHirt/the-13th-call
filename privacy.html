Privacy Policy

The 13th Call

Effective date: July 5, 2026

The 13th Call ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App may store limited gameplay information locally on your device, such as saved progress, unlocked endings, and settings preferences. This information is used only to operate the App.

2. Personal Information

The App does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location unless such features are added and disclosed in a future update.

3. Analytics and Diagnostics

If analytics or crash-reporting tools are integrated in the future, they may collect technical data such as device type, operating system version, app version, and crash logs to improve performance and stability.

4. Data Storage

Progress and settings may be stored locally on your device. If cloud saves or online account features are introduced later, this policy should be updated accordingly.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple platform services or future third-party tools. Those services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Your Choices

You can remove locally stored gameplay data by deleting the App from your device or using any in-app reset feature if available.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
